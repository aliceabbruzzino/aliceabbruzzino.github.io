Hello, I am Alice.

I am an Italian Visual Communication Designer, now enrolled in the IXUX course at the University of Limerick.
Firstly, I attended an Art High School with a specialisation in Graphic Design. There, I developed both manual and digital skills such as drawing, painting, sculpting and visual design.
After the graduation, I enrolled at Politecnico di Milano.
The first year I attended the Industrial Product course, developing manual and digital projects based on the analysis of physical products.
The following two years I moved to the Communication Design course,
in witch I graduated, developing skills on project management, brand identity, 3D modelling, motion graphics and some basic ones on web design.
I am also interested in illustration.
