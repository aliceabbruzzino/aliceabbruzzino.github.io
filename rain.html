A crazy rainy day

A wet and dramatic sound story

THE STORY

It is a peaceful rainy morning.
A lady just woke up, she gets out of bed and follows her cat downstairs to give him food. She prepares herself to go out: she moves to the bathroom and then she gets dressed.
Once out of the house, she opens the umbrella, and she walks to her car nearby. However, the rain becomes heavier and heavier and, distracted by the loud music playing, she does not pay attention to the sudden lighting strike that hits a tree making it falling down right onto her. Buried under the rubble, she tries to free herself and shout, until a rescuer helps her.
The crowd watching the scene exults while the lady has a sigh of relief.

THE GOAL

For this assignment we were required to demonstrate our ability to create a multitrack audio file from a mix of audio recordings, sounds samples and/or background music in order to design a story only made with sound effects.

01

Writing a mini-story that can be about anything.

02

03

Making a multichannel mix in Audacity, an audio editing software.

04
